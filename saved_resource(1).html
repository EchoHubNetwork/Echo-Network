Roadmap

The Echo Network was never meant to be rushed its structured to launch in clear, deliberate phases that allow for long-term scalability, community alignment, and technical reliability.
Here is the current roadmap as of Phase I:

Phase 1A: Community Foundation (Current Phase)

  • Establish and actively grow Discord and Telegram communities
  • Implement structured community moderation and support
  • Launch social media campaigns for organic growth and interaction
  • Host regular AMAs and interactive sessions for transparency and trust

Phase 1B: Platform Launch

  • Develop robust informational framework (docs, guides, FAQs)
  • Finalize branding materials, visual identity, and promotional strategies
  • Establish strategic partnerships and collaborations

Phase 2: Launch & Fair Presale

  • Deploy $ECHO token on Solana
  • Conduct 3-round presale at increasing price points
  • Lock liquidity for 90 days post-LP creation
  • Begin active community growth via content, calls, and Telegram

Phase 3: Ecosystem Formation

  • Develop and test subhub modules (EchoTrade, EchoFi, etc.)
  • Deploy claim portal with 90-day linear vesting logic
  • Create and begin early UX/UI wireframes for EchoChain
  • Visualize EchoVault metrics and tracking interfaces
  • Begin content rollout for education and branding

Phase 4: EchoChain Development

  • Begin core chain development: EVM compatibility, fee logic, vault integration
  • Initiate bridge architecture from Solana to EchoChain
  • Design subhub deployment framework for modular launch
  • Deploy developer on-chain entry logic and access fee mechanisms

Phase 5: Migration & EchoChain Launch

  • Migrate LP and $ECHO from Solana to EchoChain
  • Launch EchoChain with full vault connectivity
  • Deploy initial subhubs with $ECHO utility embedded
  • Begin vault auto-tracking and floor-price metrics
  • Public chain monitoring tools for builders and users

Phase 6: Network Maturation

  • Expand real-world and partner integrations (EchoGoods, EchoEstate)
  • Scale EchoLearn for global access and on-chain education
  • Optimize subhub interactions for economic efficiency
  • Reinforce $ECHO as the standard across the ecosystem
  • Continue absorbing value into EchoVault forever.
